Research

We are keen to collaborate on the following aspects. Please get in touch if you are interested: honghan.wu@ucl.ac.uk.

  1. externally validate our models in different settings.
  2. validate other research groups’ models in our datasets.
  3. jointly create a federated COVID-19 dataset (via a bring-model-to-data pipeline) for supporting community efforts on tackling COVID-19.
